 About Spicy Wipsy

with a name like spicy wispy you will know we do things differently.

HEAT UP YR TASTE SPICE UP YOUR LIFE*

Step - 01

heat up your taste, spice up your life.

At Spicy Wispy, we’re passionate about creating bold, flavour-packed moments. From sizzling shawarmas and oven-fresh pizzas, to hearty spaghetti, golden chips and vibrant party-jollof — we bring the delicious, the fun and the unexpected to every plate.

Born with a vision to redefine fast food with flair and quality, we source the freshest ingredients, layer in our signature spices, and serve up every dish with a side of good vibes. Whether you’re grabbing a quick bite or planning a feast, Spicy Wispy is where taste-seekers meet flavour-fans. Join us, and let’s turn everyday eats into unforgettable treats..
